# Tokyo Travel Itinerary

## Overview
A 7-day journey blending Tokyo’s cultural heritage, culinary delights, serene nature, and photography hotspots. Designed for mid-budget travelers seeking a mix of iconic landmarks and hidden gems.

---

## Daily Schedule

### Day 1: Arrival & Asakusa Culture
- **Morning/Afternoon**:  
  - Check into a mid-range hotel in **Shinjuku** or **Asakusa** ($80–$120/night).  
  - Visit **Senso-ji Temple** (Tokyo’s oldest temple) and explore **Nakamise Street** for souvenirs and snacks.  
- **Evening**:  
  - Photography session at **Tokyo Skytree** (sunset views).  
  - Dinner at a local **izakaya** (try yakitori and edamame).  

### Day 2: Historic Tokyo & Shibuya Energy
- **Morning**:  
  - **Meiji Shrine** and stroll through **Yoyogi Park**.  
  - Coffee break at **Omotesando Koffee**.  
- **Afternoon**:  
  - Explore **Harajuku** (Takeshita Street for quirky shops and street food).  
  - Head to **Shibuya Crossing** for iconic photos.  
- **Evening**:  
  - Affordable sushi at **Genki Sushi** (conveyor belt style).  
  - Drinks at **Nonbei Yokocho** (Alley of Drunkards).  

### Day 3: Day Trip to Nikko (UNESCO Heritage)
- **Full Day**:  
  - Train to **Nikko** (2 hours; ~$30 round trip).  
  - Visit **Toshogu Shrine** and **Kegon Falls**.  
  - Relax by **Lake Chuzenji**.  
- **Evening**:  
  - Return to Tokyo; casual ramen dinner.  

### Day 4: Art & Modern Culture
- **Morning**:  
  - **Ueno Park** (museums and cherry blossoms in season).  
  - Lunch at **Ameya-Yokocho Market**.  
- **Afternoon**:  
  - Explore **Akihabara** (anime shops and tech stores).  
- **Evening**:  
  - Digital art experience at **teamLab Borderless** (book tickets in advance).  

### Day 5: Relaxation & Nature
- **Morning**:  
  - **Hamarikyu Gardens** (tea ceremony at the waterfront teahouse).  
- **Afternoon**:  
  - Lunch at **Tsukiji Outer Market** (fresh seafood bowls).  
  - Unwind at **Odaiba Oedo Onsen Monogatari** (hot spring resort).  
- **Evening**:  
  - Sunset walk on **Rainbow Bridge**.  

### Day 6: Day Trip to Hakone
- **Full Day**:  
  - **Hakone Free Pass** (~$50) for transport and attractions.  
  - Cruise **Lake Ashi** with views of Mt. Fuji.  
  - Visit **Hakone Open-Air Museum**.  
- **Evening**:  
  - Stay at a **ryokan** (traditional inn; ~$150–$200 with kaiseki dinner).  

### Day 7: Final Explorations & Farewell
- **Morning**:  
  - Explore **Kagurazaka** (traditional geisha district).  
  - Lunch at a **soba noodle shop**.  
- **Afternoon**:  
  - Last-minute shopping in **Ginza** or **Don Quijote**.  
- **Evening**:  
  - Farewell dinner at **Sushi Zanmai** (mid-range sushi).  

---

## Recommendations

### Restaurants
- **Ichiran Ramen** (private booth ramen experience).  
- **Tempura Shinjuku Tsunahachi** (affordable tempura).  
- **Vegan Cafe 8ablish** (plant-based options).  

### Transportation
- Use **Suica/Pasmo cards** for trains/buses (~$5/day).  
- **JR Pass** not needed for Tokyo-only trips.  

### Local Tips
- Visit shrines early to avoid crowds.  
- Learn basic phrases like *arigatou* (thank you).  

### Cultural Considerations
- Remove shoes in traditional restaurants/ryokans.  
- Bow slightly when greeting locals.
